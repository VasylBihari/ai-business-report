# Executive Report: International Shipping vs Profitability

This project analyzes the impact of international shipping on company profitability.  
The objective was to evaluate margin dynamics, cost structure, and operational efficiency across shipping segments, and provide structured business conclusions.

## Tools & Workflow
- **Excel** – initial data review  
- **Python (Pandas)** – data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Python** – aggregation and KPI calculation  
- **OpenAI API** – structured business report formulation  
- **Python** – automated report formatting (Markdown → PDF)

The result is a management-oriented executive report with clear insights and actionable conclusions.
