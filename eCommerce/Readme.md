# eCommerce Business Performance Report

## Project Overview
This project delivers an executive-level business performance report based on an eCommerce sales dataset.  
The objective is to transform raw transactional data into **decision-ready business insights** suitable for senior management.

The report focuses on identifying revenue concentration, operational risks, and performance disparities across products, categories, platforms, and regions.

## Key Business Questions Addressed
- Where is revenue concentrated across categories, products, and brands?
- Which segments show early warning signs of underperformance?
- How do platforms and cities differ in sales efficiency and customer satisfaction?
- What areas require immediate management attention?

## Analysis Scope
The analysis is based exclusively on transactional sales data and includes:
- Sales performance by category, product, brand, platform, and city
- Order volume and revenue distribution
- Customer rating analysis as a proxy risk indicator
- Data consistency and quality checks

## Deliverables
- Executive-ready business performance report (PDF)
- Management summary with prioritized insights
- Actionable recommendations supported by quantified findings
- Data limitations and business risk assessment

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- Business analytics and executive reporting
- Metric-driven insight generation
- Data validation and consistency checks
- Translating data into management-level decisions

## Outcome
The project demonstrates an end-to-end workflow for producing a **concise, decision-oriented business report** suitable for stakeholders, business owners, and executives.
